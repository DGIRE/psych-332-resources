AI Study Tool

Google NotebookLM

NotebookLM is an AI-powered research assistant built by Google. Each notebook below has been pre-loaded with curated scientific literature on a specific topic from Module 1. You can ask it questions in plain language and it will synthesize answers grounded in the source papers, citing specific passages so you can verify everything it says.

How to use NotebookLM for this course: Open a notebook, then type a question in the chat. For example, you might ask "What does UMAP preserve that PCA does not?" or "How is gene expression measured across brain regions?" NotebookLM will answer using the research literature loaded into that notebook and point you to the exact sources. Use it to deepen your understanding of concepts, prepare for your practicum, or explore questions that go beyond lecture material.
